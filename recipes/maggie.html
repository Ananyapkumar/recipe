ALL YOU NEED

  1. Instant Nestle Maggie Pack
  2. Water
  3. Your Favorite Vegetables

METHODOLOGY

To prepare Maggie noodles, start by boiling a pot of water. Once the water reaches a rolling boil, add the Maggie noodles and cook for about 2-3 minutes or until they become soft and tender. While the noodles are cooking, you can optionally add some chopped vegetables like carrots, peas, or bell peppers to the boiling water. Drain the water, leaving just a little to keep the noodles moist. Then, add the Maggie tastemaker (seasoning) provided in the packet and stir well to mix it evenly. Cook for another minute or two, stirring occasionally, until the water evaporates, and the noodles absorb the flavors. Your delicious plate of Maggie noodles is ready to enjoy! You can garnish it with fresh herbs or grated cheese if you like.
